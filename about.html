MY STORY
Here is a little bit of my story to help you decide if I'm the right person to help you tells yours...

I have been working in the events industry ever since, working on corporate events, gala dinners, weddings and parties, and working with a high end luxury caterer. I fell in love with working on weddings, and when I got married myself in 2013; I had the most amazing day full of happy, unforgettable memories and I wanted to give others the same feeling. I completed a Diploma in Wedding and Event Planning and Design, in order to follow my dream and start up my own wedding business - Aqua's Tale was born.

I want to tell my couples story, and give them an amazing wedding day and have the best time planning it. I know what it's like to be in their shoes. 10 years in the industry has given me invaluable knowledge and expertise working on events from 20 to 500 guests. I plan and design weddings and special events in London, the South-East, and all over the UK. I am passionate about giving my clients an easy, fun planning experience while creating a celebration unique to their personality and story.
